🌸 SCRIPT

FE Transfer Script

Step 1

✨ Opening & Verification

Agent: "Hi, this is ______________. I'm following up on a life insurance quote we put together for you a little while back. Am I speaking with the person who requested the quote?"

Step 2

🔀 Identity Check

Are you speaking to the right person?

If YES

Agent: "Great. Our notes showed you just needed some time to review the details. Did you ever get a chance to get your policy in place?"

*If they have objections here, reference the Rebuttals sidebar.*

If WRONG PERSON / UNAVAILABLE

Agent: "No problem — is there a better time to reach them?"

🛑 End the call.
Step 3

📋 Data Collection

Agent: "Ok sounds good. In order to see what you qualify for we just need some basic information."

👤 What is your full Name?
📅 What is your Date of Birth?
📍 What state are you located in and your zip code?
Final Step

📞 Compliance & Transfer Setup

Agent: "Ok perfect. Looks like you may qualify for some great options in your area."

Agent: "Before I connect you, I just need to confirm a few quick things for our records."

1. First, you're okay with me transferring you to a licensed agent to go over the details — is that correct?

2. This call is being recorded for quality and compliance purposes — are you alright with that?

3. Can I confirm the best phone number to reach you is [number on file], in case we get disconnected? And you agree to us calling you back?

⚠️ MUST RECEIVE YES TO ALL ABOVE BEFORE TRANSFERRING
🚀

Live Agent Assistance

⭐ FE Positioning
“The biggest mistake most families make is waiting until health changes or rates increase.”
“Final expense coverage is designed to protect your loved ones from financial stress during an already difficult time.”
“Most people aren’t looking for millions of dollars — they just want dignity, peace of mind, and to make sure their family is okay.”
“Even a small policy can make a huge difference for a family.”
“The goal is protection that actually fits your budget long term.”
“A policy only works if it stays affordable.”
“We shop multiple companies because no one carrier is best for everyone.”
“A lot of people are surprised they qualify for more than they expected.”
“The licensed specialist’s job is to help you understand your options — not pressure you.”
🛡️ Rebuttals
If "I already got covered":

“That's great to hear — glad you're protected. Mind if I ask who you went with, just so I can update our file?” (If it's vague or they hesitate, it may be a brush-off; you can gently re-engage: "While I've got you, would it be worth a comparison to make sure you're not overpaying?")

If "Not yet / I forgot / I got busy":

“Totally understand, that's exactly why I'm following up. Let me just confirm a few details are still accurate so I can give you an up-to-date number — this'll take about a minute.”

If "Not interested":

“Understood, I appreciate your time. I'll make a note. Have a good one.” (If they ask to be removed, confirm you'll take them off the list.)

If Declined OR "I Already Tried":

“Yes I understand and I hear that 50% of the time, many people are surprised to learn that being declined by one carrier doesn't necessarily mean you're uninsurable and getting a guaranteed issue plan or simple issue plan will get you some life insurance history and we can ask that they shop plans and more coverage semi annually. By doing so you agree that you consent to be contacted if we can offer a lower rate or better benefits for the same type plan? That you had made an inquiry and during my call back from your initial inquiry, would you mind telling the agent your satisfactory rate?”

If skeptical:

“That’s fair — this is exactly why we review everything carefully before recommending anything.”

If worried about cost:

“We’re not trying to create another bill — the goal is to find something comfortable and sustainable.”

If “I already talked to someone”:

“Absolutely, and this is simply a second review to make sure nothing was missed and that you’re getting the best value available.”

If “I need to think about it”:

“Of course — most people do. That’s why we at least make sure you have accurate information and real numbers first.”

If older lead:

“A lot of people requested information months ago and life just got busy. We’re simply following up to see what still makes sense for you today.”
