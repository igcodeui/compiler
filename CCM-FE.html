🌸 SCRIPT

Final Expense Call Flow

Step 1

✨ The Pattern-Interrupter Opening

Agent: Hi, is this (Customer Name)?

Prospect: Yes, who is this?

Agent: Hi (Customer Name), this is (Your Name). I'm calling because I have an alert here, in regards to your Life insurance policy.

🛑 CRITICAL PAUSE Stop talking completely for 1 to 2 seconds. Let the word "alert" sink in. Then say:

Agent: ...Are you there?

Step 2

🔀 The Fork in the Road

How did the prospect respond to the opening?

Track A: No Policy / Fine

Agent: Exactly, (Customer Name), and that’s why it flagged an alert. Our system shows you don't have any life insurance active right now to back up your health coverage. Let’s just check your request real quick:

  • • Were you just looking to make sure your kids aren't stuck paying for a $10,000 funeral out of pocket?
  • • If something happened tomorrow, would they have to pay for it out of their own savings?
  • • To see what plans you qualify for when you clicked the link, you're still located in (State), correct?
  • • And you are managing to take care of yourself at home right now, no nursing home care?
🌉 The Responsibility Bridge

Got it. Now, when you looked this up online, it asked who you wanted to protect. If something happened unexpectedly...

Track B: Already Covered

Agent: Got it, and that explains the alert. The system flagged it to make sure your current policy actually covers a modern funeral, since inflation has made a lot of older plans too small. Let's look at your request:

  • • Is your current policy enough to cover a $10,000 funeral today, or did you want a little extra cushion so your family isn't short?
  • • Is your plan a permanent whole life policy, or is it a term plan that expires when you get older?
  • • Does your payment stay exactly the same forever, or does the price go up?
🌉 The Responsibility Bridge

Perfect. Since you already have a plan, you know how this works. If something happened unexpectedly...

Step 3

🔍 Discovery & Emotional Tie-Down

Agent: ...Who would be the one handling things for you? Your (Son/Daughter/Spouse)?

Have you talked to them about it, or would they be figuring it out on the fly?

Look, that’s exactly why we’re fixing this alert. When someone passes, the funeral home demands money immediately. If there's no plan, your family has to make heavy emotional choices while scrambling for cash. Since you want (Beneficiary Name) handling things, would you rather leave them a solid plan, or leave them stuck with the bill?

Step 4

🩺 Health Qualification

Agent: Now I am going to ask a few health questions so I can see which companies would approve you and which one gives you the best rate.

🚬 Do you currently use tobacco or nicotine?
⚖️ What is your height and weight?
❤️ Any heart attack, stroke, cancer, COPD, diabetes, oxygen use, kidney issues, or hospital stays recently?
💊 Any medications you take regularly?
🏥 Any home health care, nursing home care, or mobility assistance?
🩺 Any recent surgeries, pending tests, or upcoming procedures?
Step 5

📊 Present Three Options

Agent: Based on your age and health, here are the three options that make the most sense:

Basic Option

$____ / mo

Helps with immediate costs, but may not cover everything.

Standard Option

Best balance of protection and affordability for most families.

Stronger Protection

More breathing room for funeral costs, final bills, and family support.

Agent: Most families choose the middle option because it gives solid protection without overdoing the monthly payment. Would the $10,000 option be comfortable for you, or do you want to keep it closer to the basic option?

Step 6

📝 Assumptive Close Into Application

Agent: Good choice. That gives your family real protection without making the payment uncomfortable. Let us get the application started and see if we can get you approved today. I will walk you through it. It is simple. To confirm approval, underwriting just needs the basic application details:

  • 🔹 First, I need your full legal name exactly as it appears on your ID.
  • 🔹 What is your date of birth?
  • 🔹 What is your current mailing address?
  • 🔹 Who would you want listed as the beneficiary? What is their relationship to you?
  • 🔹 What is the best phone number for you?
  • 🔹 For the payment draft, what day of the month is usually best? Most people choose the day after they receive Social Security or their monthly income.
Final Step

✅ Final Confirmation

Agent: So just to confirm, the goal here is to make sure (Beneficiary) is not left trying to come up with money at the worst possible time. We are going with the $________ coverage at $________ per month. The rate is designed to stay level, the coverage does not decrease, and as long as the premium is paid, this stays in place for your family. Let us finish the application and get the approval confirmed.
